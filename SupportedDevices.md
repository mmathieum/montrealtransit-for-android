We support all Android devices with screen from 2" to 10.1", with or without hardware keyboard, running any Android 1.5 or higher.

**Obviously, this device list is out of date. We do support all Android devices released in Canada.**

Because Android is _open and free_, we also support Android version from the _community_ (like the popular [CyanogenMod](http://www.cyanogenmod.com/)).

We don't have all these devices but we are testing each release on:
  * the _oldest_ Android 1.5 smartphone with hardware keyboard available in Canada (the Rogers HTC Dream)
  * a 5" tablet running Android 1.6 (the Archos 5 IT)
  * a mid-range smartphone (the HTC Magic) running Android 2.1 from the community (CyanogenMod-5)
  * the _best_ Android 2.2 smartphone (the Google Nexus One).
We think that covers pretty much all Android devices in the market.
